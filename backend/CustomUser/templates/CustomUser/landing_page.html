Who we are:

  • Users can report instances of waste accumulation or improper disposal by uploading photos and providing descriptions.
  • The app includes an interactive map that displays waste accumulation hotspots, recycling centers, and other relevant locations. Users can view reported waste incidents and points of interest on the map.
  • The app utilizes machine learning algorithms to predict future waste generation patterns. This helps in planning waste management strategies and optimizing collection routes.
  • Users can join community cleaning events and volunteer for waste collection activities. The app provides information about upcoming events and allows users to register their participation.
  • The app encourages users to contribute to reforestation efforts by earning virtual points or rewards through their waste reporting and participation in waste management activities. These points can be used to support tree-planting initiatives.
  • The app features a leaderboard that ranks users based on their contributions to waste management activities. Users earn points for reporting waste, participating in cleaning events, and planting trees, fostering healthy competition and recognition.

Mission

"To enhance waste management in Nepal by delivering efficient solutions through collaboration with the government, international partners, and communities, while striving for innovative and environmentally positive approaches."

Vision

Providing sustainable waste solutions in Nepal, promoting eco-friendly practices, creating jobs, and partnering for better waste management.
